WHAT A YEAR!

2022 proved to be a wonderful year indeed. Didn't it? First, it started with the union of the 'Ada' of the house with her heartthrob, LESLIE 😍.The celebrations were maaaad! We killed it!💃 As if that was not enough, the 'Diokpa' followed suit. 😆 Once again, the celebrations were lit. 🔥 🔥

But the marriages weren't all that made the year wonderful🙅In the financial aspect, we grew as well📈.We closed the year with some one hundred and sixty-nine thousand Naira (₦ 169,000) profit made from FX investments💰 💲. Another big one was our mama finally retired from work after thirty five years(?) Amazing! And as if that wasn't enough good news, she has also begun to make good progress in her poultry farm business, purchasing a whooping 150 layers and recording as high as 103 laid eggs in a day! 🙌

What about Nma? She was able to finalize her university clearance and is now awaiting her NYSC call up🥁. Now, recall that the last person that went for NYSC in the house came back with a life partner.🙊 Okay, are you thinking what I am thinking 😉 or should we all mind our businesses?😏

And just when you may be thinking that I am done, this year, we welcomed a baby boy too🤸 🤸 🤸 Yoma came into the building and wooah! That boy is a fine baller. No capping at all. The only thing we are yet to settle is "does he look more like his mom or like his dad?" Me, I think he resembles his mother more sha. Mr Leslie would need to show us his baby pictures before he can win this argument🤧 Anyway, welcome to the family, Yomalicious Yoma Chukwuemeka Akpojotor!

Well, like they say, count your blessings, name them one by one and it will surprise you what the Lord has done. We cannot really count how much blessings we received in 2022. All we can do is show gratitude to the one who made all this possible. 2023, we pray, would be much better. Amen! So, this is cheers to a better 2023 🥂
